Secretolytic Effect of Ambroxol

Secretolytic effect: ambroxol lowers
mucus viscosity2,10

Ambroxol has a normalizing effect on the
serous and mucus secretion of the bronchial
epithelium, which promotes a reduction of
mucus viscosity, thereby facilitating mucus
clearance.2,10
