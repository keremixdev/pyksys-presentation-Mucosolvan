Protective effect: ambroxol protects against the formation of new mucus, and reduces oxidative stress in the lungs

Ambroxol increases the availability of surfactant and improves mucus quality, which helps protect against the formation and accumulation of new mucus in the lungs.10

In addition, ambroxol possesses anti-oxidative and anti-inflammatory properties which offer protection against oxidative stress by free radicals.10
